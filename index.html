Aadhya Kumar

Founder

Hi! I’m Aadhya Kumar, a junior at the International Academy Okma Campus. Growing up with a brachial plexus injury shaped how I see resilience, community, and possibility. I created Neuro-Networks to raise awareness, support research, and empower young people with disabilities.

I practice Taekwondo, serve as Head Delegate in Model UN, give anatomy lectures in Pre-Med Club, and am involved in IASA. I’m passionate about leadership and medicine intersecting to create impact.
