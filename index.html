Our Mission

Neuro-Networks is dedicated to educating youth, raising awareness and advocacy for brachial plexus injury research, and empowering individuals with brachial plexus injuries to live without limits through education, community, and representation.

Why Does This Matter?

Brachial plexus injuries remain underrepresented in public education and research awareness. Many affected youth face stigma, misunderstanding, and limited peer support. Neuro-Networks bridges the gap between medical knowledge and youth understanding through education, advocacy, and empowerment.

Brachial Plexus Injury by the Numbers

1–2 per 1,000

Infants are affected by brachial plexus birth injuries in the United States annually.

30,000+

Traumatic brachial plexus injuries occur each year in the U.S., often due to accidents or sports injuries.

20–30%

Low Awareness
