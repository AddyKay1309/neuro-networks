Leadership

Aadhya Kumar

Founder | Junior – International Academy

Growing up with a brachial plexus injury shaped Aadhya’s perspective on resilience, advocacy, and leadership. She founded Neuro-Networks to raise awareness, support research, and empower youth with disabilities.

Involved in Taekwondo, Model UN (Head Delegate), Pre-Med Club lectures, and IASA. Passionate about medicine, neuroscience, and impact-driven leadership.

Aarya Verma

Co-Founder | Junior – International Academy

Aarya focuses on biomedical innovation and fundraising strategy for Neuro-Networks.
