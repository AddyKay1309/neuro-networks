Aadhya Kumar — Founder

Aadhya founded Neuro-Networks to expand awareness and representation for individuals living with brachial plexus injuries. Growing up with BPI shaped her understanding of resilience, leadership, and advocacy. In addition to leading Neuro-Networks, she serves as a Head Delegate in Model United Nations, practices Taekwondo, and delivers anatomy lectures through her school’s Pre-Med Club. She is passionate about bridging medicine, youth leadership, and systemic change.
